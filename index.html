About Us

About Us

Alpha Kappa Rho (AKR) Fraternity and Sorority, also known as the Akrho, is a fraternity established in the Philippines. It was founded on October 4, 1973, at the University of Santo Tomas in Manila, Philippines, by students led by late Bro. Salvador Lacsina Jr. The fraternity was established with the core principles of promoting camaraderie, brotherhood, academic excellence, and service to the community. Over the years, AKR has played a significant role in shaping the lives of its members, providing them with opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and social responsibility. Since its founding, AKR has grown to become one of the largest and most influential fraternities in the Philippines, with chapters and members across the country and even abroad. The fraternity has been actively involved in various community service projects, charitable activities, and advocacies aimed at making a positive impact on society. With a strong commitment to its founding principles and a rich history of brotherhood and service, Alpha Kappa Rho continues to thrive as an organization dedicated to empowering its members and contributing to the betterment of society.

Our Objectives, Goals, and Advocacy

Objectives:

Alpha Kappa Rho Fraternity and Sorority, also known as the Akrho, is dedicated to achieving several objectives that reflect its core values and principles:

  • Promote loyalty, unity, and integrity among members.
  • Foster brotherhood and sisterhood, creating strong bonds and friendships.
  • Promote academic excellence, leadership development, and personal growth.
  • Engage in humanitarian projects and community service initiatives.
  • Promote ethical conduct, social responsibility, and a commitment to serving others.

Goals:

Our fraternity has set several goals to guide our actions and initiatives:

  • Provide opportunities for members to develop leadership skills and achieve academic success.
  • Conduct meaningful community service projects that address pressing social issues and improve lives.
  • Promote a culture of inclusivity, diversity, and respect within the fraternity and in society at large.
  • Collaborate with other organizations and stakeholders to amplify our impact and reach.
  • Foster a supportive and empowering environment where members can thrive personally and professionally.

Advocacy:

Alpha Kappa Rho is deeply committed to advocating for positive change and social justice. Our advocacy efforts focus on:

  • Empowering marginalized communities and promoting equity and inclusion.
  • Advocating for education access and opportunities for underprivileged youth.
  • Raising awareness about important social issues such as poverty, inequality, and environmental sustainability.
  • Supporting initiatives that promote peace, justice, and human rights locally and globally.
  • Encouraging civic engagement and activism among our members and the broader community.
